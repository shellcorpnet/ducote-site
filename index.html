What we do

Full-spectrum support for rural public projects.

From establishing a need to final closeout, we handle the technical, regulatory, and administrative lift so our clients can focus on serving their communities.

01

Grant Writing & Administration

Strategic funding plans and execution, with a proven record of more than $50M in grant wins — known for attention to detail, financial accountability, and creative problem solving.

02

Project Development

End-to-end support from scoping and timelines through clearing every regulatory hurdle, including narratives, documentation, and project coordination.

03

Environmental Reporting

NEPA and SERP report processes, working regularly with SHPO, DSL, DEQ, ODFW, Army Corps, USFS and other regulatory agencies.

04

Labor Standards Compliance

Davis-Bacon and Oregon BOLI monitoring on Prevailing Wage Rate jobs, including Certified Payroll Report review and on-site interviews.

05

Equity Consulting (DEIA)

DEI plans, equity language for grant proposals, and MWESB outreach policies that open up more funding opportunities.

06

Community Development Block Grants

Deep CDBG specialization with over $25M in written and administered CDBG projects — water, sewer, libraries, fire stations, senior centers, and community centers.
